Online Clinic

If you feel you need to talk to a Doctor, or maybe another Healthcare Profressional, We have a great team of them ready to deal with you in a private one to one consultation. Please fill out the form below or upload a voice note which will be heard by one of the team and dealt with swiftly. You will then receive a text or email whichever you prefer telling you to book a convenient appointment slot. If your diagnosis means you are prescribed or recommended a medication or supplement, this can be supplied by either our Pharmacy team or bought from the Wellbeing Shop.
